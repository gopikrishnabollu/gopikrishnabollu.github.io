RGUKT

Catering to the Educational Needs of Gifted Rural Youth of Telangana

The institute offers six-year Bachelor of Technology after 10th class examinations which includes two years pre-university course equivalent to intermediate degree followed by four year degree course in engineering course.The college comes under autonomous Institutions specialized in teaching and research sector in Information Technology and engineering. The institution follows the norms of its own and does not follow regulations of Government of India, University Grants Commission (UGC) India.




How to Apply?

The candidates who wish to seek admission into RGUKT should appear for POLYCET-2021 conducted by SBTET

The State Board of Technical Education and Training (SBTET), Telangana, Hyderabad will conduct the Entrance Examination for the academic year 2021-22 of RGUKT along with other Engineering Polytechnics of the State Government as per the rules and regulations of SBTET. However, filling up of seats, admission and counseling procedure in respect of 6-Year Integrated B.Tech Program offered by RGUKT will be as per the norms of RGUKT only and the counseling for the said program will be conducted by RGUKT. Detailed prospectus, seat position and fee structure etc. will be placed on the University website.
